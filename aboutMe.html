About Me

I am a highly ambitious student in the final semester of my Computer Science and Mathematics Bachelor's degree, with a focus on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Throughout my academic journey, I have consistently demonstrated a remarkable ability to quickly grasp new concepts and excel in various areas of study. I am known for my strong teamwork skills, meticulous attention to detail, and self-discipline. Additionally, I possess excellent interpersonal communication skills, allowing me to collaborate effectively with others.
My native language is Hebrew and I am fluent in English.

I have a strong passion for volunteering and have dedicated a significant part of my life to community service. During my national service, I provided assistance at a school for at-risk teenagers, where I was able to make a positive impact on their lives. Moreover, for the past eight years, I have been actively involved with a charitable organization that specializes in the collection and distribution of essential supplies, including food, to families in need. Throughout my high school years, I also participated in the 'Mashatzim' program, initially as an apprentice and later as a leader. This experience allowed me to develop leadership skills while contributing to meaningful projects. In my final year of high school, I furthered my commitment to mentoring by completing a Mentoring course at Sapir College and providing guidance to an at-risk apprentice.
